Meet Heena Singhal

Author Visual Artist

Heena Singhal is a Writer, Poet and a Visual Artist.

Her first historical-fiction novel, Songs of the Reed, set in New Delhi, Spain & Kashmir, has
won her an Debut Novelist Award by HT City.

She is an advocate of women & children’s rights, inclusivity, and these themes often reflect in her works.

A Chartered Accountant by education, she provides soft skills training at the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India. Her keen interest in Children’s literature has lead her to write and illustrate eight children's books.

Please Note :

Heena accepts and promotes brands that align with her beliefs.
She does not send out free copies of her book for review.
However, she conducts regular giveaways of her book
and artwork through her Instagram Page.
